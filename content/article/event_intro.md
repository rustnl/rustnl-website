RustNL is the organizer of [*RustWeek*](https://rustweek.org/), the week-long event for the Rust Community, the Rust Project and professional Rust users.

<!-- more -->

![Main stage at RustWeek 2025](/images/main-stage-rustweek2025.jpg)

As part of RustWeek we organize the Rust Project All-Hands, the event where members of the project and contributors meet and work together on Rust's future. In 2025 the All-Hands included the 10 year celebration of stable Rust:

![10 year celebration of stable Rust](/images/10y.jpg)

We occasionally organize Rust meetups in varying cities in the Netherlands, see [meetup.com/rust-nederland](https://meetup.com/rust-nederland).
